Noodles

Delicious and easy noodles recipe

This delicious and easy to make noodles recipe is a dish that you MUST try!

The steps are very easy and you most likely already have the ingredients in your kitchen.
So what are you waiting for?

Ingredients

  • 1 Pack of instant noodles
  • Half an onion
  • 2 Gloves of garlic
  • 2 TSP Soy sauce
  • 1 Chicken breast

Steps

  1. First, cut the chicken breast into small pieces.
  2. Put the chicken in a pot.
  3. Chop your onion and garlic, then add them to the pot
  4. Mix until the chicken and onion get a golden color
  5. Add the soy sauce
  6. Add a cup of water
  7. When the water starts boiling, add the noodles
  8. Let it cook for three minutes then add the noodles' spices
