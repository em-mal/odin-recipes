Noodles

Delicious and easy noodles recipe

This delicious and easy to make noodles recipe is a dish that you MUST try!

The steps are very easy and you most likely already have the ingredients in your kitchen.
So what are you waiting for?

Ingredients

  • 1 Pack of instant noodles
  • Half an onion
  • 2 Gloves of garlic
  • 2 TSP Soy sauce
  • 1 Chicken breast
